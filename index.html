Scientific Research and Community is an Independent Open Access Publishers and encourages all academic disciplines to publish their research globally. It is a platform for scientific researchers and young researchers to promote their research values. Scientific Research and Community is a non-profit organization that welcomes people from various research backgrounds, including Medical, Clinical, Healthcare, Engineering, Life Sciences, Pharmacy, and more. The organization aims to publish original research work that contributes significantly to enhance scientific knowledge in various fields. Scientific Research and Community focuses on Clinical Research, Epidemiology, Oncology, Biomedicine, Dentistry, Medical Education, and many other areas. The organization collaborates with well-qualified community and advisory board members to facilitate research and development. Their areas of interest include Earth & Environmental Sciences, Pharmaceutical Research, Engineering, and Technology.

About Us

Scientific Research and Community Ltd is an independent open access publisher that encourages research in various disciplines. We aim to enhance scientific knowledge and promote collaboration among researchers.
